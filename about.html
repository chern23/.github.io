About

The story begins in Þingvellir (Thingvellir) National Park, where the only visible rift valley exists on land right in Iceland. I could see the North American tectonic plate meet the Eurasian tectonic plate; I was astounded. I needed to take a piece of this memory and this park with me so I found a flat, rune-like keychain that would forever serve as a reminder. From then on, I have sought out small pieces that best represent the places that I have travelled to (at least to me). It is these very keychains that inspired me to now share my photos and experiences of each location. Please enjoy your travels through the chains of my memories.
